## Figure 1 | Animal meta-synthesis establishes the Sweet Trap reward–fitness decoupling gradient (Δ_ST) across eight non-human species.

**(a)** Random-effects meta-analysis (DerSimonian–Laird) of eight independently confirmed Sweet Trap cases spanning six phyla. Each point shows the standardised reward–fitness decoupling gradient Δ_ST with 95% confidence intervals. Blue circles denote Route A (ancestral mismatch); orange circles denote Route B (novel stimulus hijack). The diamond represents the pooled random-effects estimate (Δ_ST = 0.72 [0.60, 0.83]; I² = 67%, τ² = 0.031). **(b)** Subgroup meta-analysis by F3 persistence mechanism reveals significant moderating effects (between-group Q = 12.4, p = 0.006, explaining 51% of residual I²). Individual habit (M1) and acute mortality (M4) mechanisms show the highest Δ_ST severity; trans-generational mechanisms (M2+M3) show the lowest. Dashed line marks pooled estimate. **(c)** Meta-regression of Δ_ST on log(τ_env/τ_adapt), the environmental-to-adaptive timescale ratio (Proposition P3). Bubble size encodes random-effects weight. The positive but non-significant slope (β = +0.03 per log unit, p = 0.35, N = 6) is underpowered at current sample sizes; the prediction is not falsified.

**图1 | 动物荟萃分析建立了跨物种甜蜜陷阱回报–适应性解耦梯度（Δ_ST）。**
**(a)** 涵盖八个案例的随机效应荟萃分析（DerSimonian–Laird）。蓝色圆点为A路径（祖先失配），橙色圆点为B路径（新奇刺激劫持）。菱形为汇总估计（Δ_ST = 0.72 [0.60, 0.83]）。**(b)** F3机制亚组分析：个体习惯（M1）与急性死亡（M4）机制的Δ_ST最高，跨代机制（M2+M3）最低。**(c)** Δ_ST对log(τ_env/τ_adapt)的荟萃回归；气泡大小编码随机效应权重。正向斜率未达显著水平（β = +0.03，p = 0.35），样本量不足但P3命题未被证伪。

## Figure 2 | Human Sweet Traps: four confirmed cases across luxury goods, housing, investment, and short-video consumption.

**(a)** Luxury goods (C5): time-series comparison of per-capita luxury expenditure share (thick line, 95% CI band) between high-SES households (top income quartile; blue) and population mean (grey), 2000–2020. Δ_ST = +0.114 [+0.101, +0.127]; estimated 120 million households persistently misallocating. **(b)** Luxury goods welfare gradient: bitter effect of excess luxury spending on reported subjective well-being (SWB) by income quartile, controlling for absolute spending level. Bottom-quartile households show the most negative welfare trajectory (β = −0.18, p < 0.001). **(c)** Housing over-purchase (C13): event-study of SWB following housing expenditure exceeding 40% of annual income. T = 0 marks purchase year; error bars show 95% CIs from stacked DiD. SWB rises transiently then declines below pre-purchase baseline by T+3 (β = −0.092, p = 0.003). **(d)** Left: cross-domain Δ_ST estimates for confirmed human Sweet Traps (C8 investment, C5 luxury, C12 short-video, C13 housing). Right: lock-in persistence coefficient ρ (first-order autocorrelation of excess engagement) for the same four cases. Short-video (C12) exhibits both high Δ_ST and the highest ρ (0.759), flagging it as highest-severity human Sweet Trap.

**图2 | 人类甜蜜陷阱：奢侈品、住房、投资与短视频四个确认案例。**
**(a)** 奢侈品（C5）：高SES家庭与总体均值的奢侈品支出份额时序对比，Δ_ST = +0.114 [+0.101, +0.127]，估计1.2亿家庭持续错配资源。**(b)** 奢侈品福利梯度：超额奢侈品支出对主观幸福感的苦涩效应按收入四分位数分组，最低收入组福利损失最为显著（β = −0.18，p < 0.001）。**(c)** 住房过度购入（C13）事件研究：购入后第3年主观幸福感降至购前基线以下（β = −0.092，p = 0.003）。**(d)** 左：四个确认人类案例的Δ_ST估计；右：锁定持续系数ρ。短视频（C12）兼具高Δ_ST与最高ρ（0.759），是严重程度最高的人类甜蜜陷阱。

## Figure 3 | Cross-cultural universality: internet-age environments outpace cultural adaptation, predicting Sweet Trap severity across 52 nations.

**(a)** Scatter plot of country-level aggregate Sweet Trap severity (Σ_ST; y-axis) against log(τ_env/τ_adapt) — the ratio of internet environmental transition speed to composite cultural rigidity (Gelfand 2011 tightness score) — for n = 31 countries with Gelfand data (plotted points). The regression line and 95% confidence band derive from the primary model using a composite τ_adapt index (Gelfand + Hofstede UAI/LTO; n = 52, β = −0.295 [−0.611, −0.046], HC3-robust SE; p = 0.043, R² = 0.11). Countries coloured by region (Paul Tol 7-colour colorblind-safe palette). China (large orange circle) sits at the 1st percentile of τ_env GDP doubling time (world's fastest economic environment), 72nd percentile of cultural tightness, yet Cantril ladder residual at only the 21st percentile — welfare below GDP/Gini/life-expectancy predictions, consistent with P3. **(b)** Specification curve across 10 regression variants differing in τ_env operationalisation (internet-based: 5 specs; GDP-doubling-time: 5 specs) and τ_adapt measure (Gelfand, Hofstede UAI, Hofstede LTO, composite). Filled circles: p < 0.10; open circles: p ≥ 0.10. Result: 5/10 specs significantly negative (p < 0.10), 0/10 significantly positive; 7/10 directionally negative. Internet-based τ_env yields consistently negative estimates across all four cultural rigidity operationalisations; GDP-based τ_env is uniformly null — isolating the novel technological signal (not general economic growth) as the relevant environmental timescale. **(c)** China's global percentile rank on the five key P3 variables. GDP doubling speed at the 1st percentile (fastest globally) contrasts with a 21st-percentile happiness gap (below-predicted welfare), consistent with extreme timescale mismatch driving Sweet Trap formation. Aggregate Σ_ST at the 53rd percentile (median) masks the within-person Sweet Trap dynamics detected in the CFPS individual panel (Layer B).

**图3 | 跨文化普遍性：互联网时代环境变迁速率超越文化适应速率，预测52国甜蜜陷阱严重程度。**

**(a)** 国家级甜蜜陷阱综合严重程度（Σ_ST）与log(τ_env/τ_adapt)的散点图（n=31，含格尔方文化紧密度数据）。主模型回归线来自完整52国复合τ_adapt规格（β = −0.295 [−0.611, −0.046]，p = 0.043，R² = 0.11）。中国（橙色大圆点）位于GDP倍增时间第1百分位（全球最快），文化紧密度第72百分位，而Cantril幸福感残差仅处第21百分位，与P3预测一致。**(b)** 10个回归规约的规格曲线：互联网型τ_env（5条）与GDP倍增型τ_env（5条）。结果：5/10显著负向（p<0.10），0/10显著正向，7/10方向负向；互联网型τ_env在所有文化刚性测量下均为显著负向，GDP型均为零效应——印证技术性信号（而非经济增长本身）的理论意义。**(c)** 中国在P3关键变量中的全球百分位排名：GDP倍增速度第1百分位（世界最快）与幸福感残差第21百分位（低于GDP/基尼/预期寿命预测）并存，支持极端时间尺度失配驱动甜蜜陷阱形成的理论。综合Σ_ST处第53百分位（中位数），但掩盖了Layer B CFPS个体面板中检测到的人内甜蜜陷阱动态。

## Figure 4 | Discriminant validity: five boundary cases demonstrate construct specificity of the Sweet Trap.

**(a)** C2 ("鸡娃" intensive child education): The F2 endorsement condition fails — education expenditure share decreases with parental SES (cor = −0.093), indicating coerced rather than voluntary participation. This misclassifies C2 as a boundary-condition case rather than a Sweet Trap. **(b)** C4 (marriage market): Δ_ST is negative across all welfare outcomes (life satisfaction: −0.039 [−0.143, +0.060]; happiness: −0.075 [−0.190, +0.034]; marital satisfaction: −0.110 [−0.215, −0.002]). Wrong-sign Δ_ST likely reflects measurement limitations; the bride-price payer's utility is unobserved in CGSS. **(c)** C6 (health supplements): F3 peer norm effect is strong (β = +0.729, p < 10⁻¹⁷⁰) and bitter effect is confirmed (ΔLDL = +0.771 [+0.306, +1.235]), but the subjective reward signal (θ) is null (β = −0.0009, p = 0.74), indicating an incomplete Sweet Trap signature — F3 present, F1 absent. **(d)** D3 (996 overwork): Sweet (job satisfaction) shows β = −0.074 [−0.103, −0.044] (p = 1.00 one-sided), falsifying F1; only 10.2% of 432 specification-curve variants yield positive coefficients. Classified as coerced exposure. **(e)** D_alcohol: tripartite F2 decomposition. Type A (aspirational) shows SES-positive correlations (F2 PASS), qualifying as a Sweet Trap candidate. Type C (dependent) shows SES-negative correlations (F2 FAIL), indicating addiction rather than Sweet Trap. **(f)** Diagnostic heatmap summarising F1–F3 pass/fail across all five boundary cases.

**图4 | 判别效度：五个边界案例论证甜蜜陷阱的构念特异性。**
**(a)** C2鸡娃：F2背书条件失败，SES与教育支出份额负相关（cor = −0.093），属于强制参与而非自愿。**(b)** C4婚姻市场：Δ_ST全为负值，可能源于聘礼支付方效用不可观测的测量限制。**(c)** C6保健品：F3同伴效应显著，苦涩效应确认，但主观回报信号θ为零（p = 0.74），F1缺失，签名不完整。**(d)** D3 996加班：Sweet方向β完全反转（β = −0.074，p = 1.00），432条规约曲线中仅10.2%为正，属强制暴露。**(e)** D_alcohol：三分法F2分解，仅A型（志愿性）满足背书条件，C型（依赖性）判定为成瘾而非甜蜜陷阱。**(f)** F1–F3诊断矩阵汇总。

## Figure 5 | Grand synthesis: Sweet Trap severity (Σ_ST) as a universal function of reward–fitness decoupling and timescale mismatch.

**(a)** All confirmed Sweet Trap cases (triangles: human Layer B; circles: animal Layer A) plotted in the two-dimensional conceptual space of environmental-to-adaptive timescale ratio [log(τ_env/τ_adapt)] versus cumulative Sweet Trap severity (Σ_ST = Δ_ST × τ_F3 × (1 − feedback)). Bubble size encodes F2 endorsement strength. Three zones are demarcated: fast-cycle hijack (algorithmic, C12, upper left), cultural Sweet Trap (human-specific, central), and classic ecological trap (animal-dominant, right). The anchor case A5 (ICSS brain stimulation) is marked with a star; it lacks a defined τ_env because stimulation frequency is laboratory-controlled. Layer A trend line shown with 95% CI. Dashed horizontal line marks the Σ_ST = 0.5 severity threshold. **(b)** Stacked decomposition of Σ_ST by contributing F-condition (F1 decoupling, F3 self-reinforcement, F4 feedback blockade) for eight selected cases, ordered by total Σ_ST. Layer A cases (right four bars) consistently show higher F1 contributions; Layer B cases (left four bars) show higher F3 and F4 contributions, reflecting the role of cultural institutions and market feedback opacity in sustaining human Sweet Traps.

**图5 | 大综合图：甜蜜陷阱严重程度（Σ_ST）作为时间尺度失配的普适函数。**
**(a)** 所有确认案例绘制于log(τ_env/τ_adapt)与Σ_ST二维空间。三个区域分别对应快速循环劫持（算法性，左上）、文化甜蜜陷阱（人类特异，中部）和经典生态陷阱（动物主导，右侧）。锚点案例A5（ICSS）因τ_env实验室受控而无法定义，单独标注。水平虚线标记Σ_ST = 0.5严重程度阈值。**(b)** 8个代表案例的Σ_ST F条件分解堆叠图：Layer A案例F1贡献较高；Layer B案例F3与F4贡献突出，反映文化制度与市场反馈不透明对人类甜蜜陷阱的维持作用。

## Figure 6 | Mendelian randomization establishes causal genetic architecture across three Sweet Trap sub-classes (Layer D).

**(a)** Two-sample Mendelian randomization forest plot for seven causal chains spanning three Sweet Trap sub-classes and two validity controls. Each chain displays three estimators: IVW (solid circle), weighted median (solid square), and MR-Egger (triangle). Horizontal lines show 95% confidence intervals; the vertical line marks OR = 1 (null). Background shading demarcates sub-class groups. *Engineered Sweet Trap* (red): genetic propensity to risk-taking causally increases depression risk (IVW OR = 1.38 [1.18, 1.62], p = 4.9×10⁻⁵, n = 25 instruments) and antidepressant use (OR = 1.40 [1.18, 1.65], p = 9.8×10⁻⁵); weighted median concordant in both chains; MR-Egger intercepts clean (p = 0.16, p = 0.24). *Ancestral-mismatch Sweet Trap* (orange): genetic propensity to higher alcohol intake causally elevates alcoholic liver disease (OR = 5.41 [2.76, 10.57], p = 8.2×10⁻⁷, n = 90 instruments), establishing the causal Bitter side of D_alcohol that within-person behavioural analysis could not detect; genetic propensity to higher BMI causally doubles Type-2 diabetes risk (OR = 2.06 [1.61, 2.65], p = 1.6×10⁻⁸, n = 90 instruments), homologous to the Drosophila TAS1R/Gr64 axis (Layer A case A4, Δ_ST = +0.71). *Discriminant validity* (green, **PROTECTIVE direction**): genetic propensity to more years of schooling causally *decreases* depression risk (OR = 0.88 [0.81, 0.97], p = 0.006, n = 203 instruments) — demonstrating that not all aspirational deferred-reward behaviours are Sweet Traps; education fails F1 (reward–fitness decoupling) because educational investment is calibrated to current-environment fitness. Chain 7 (smoking → alcoholic liver) is shown in grey with dashed intervals; MR-Egger intercept p = 0.009 indicates horizontal pleiotropy in the addiction-cluster genetic architecture and this chain is reported in SI only. **(b)** Sensitivity panel: Cochran Q p-values (left column, red = Q p < 0.001 indicating heterogeneity; teal = Q p > 0.05) and MR-Egger intercept p-values (right column, teal CLEAN = p > 0.10; red FLAG = p < 0.10) for each completed chain. Five of seven chains are clean on Egger pleiotropy. **(c)** Cross-species molecular bridges: the human GWAS loci driving the MR instruments map onto animal homologs from Layer A. TAS1R2/TAS1R3 ↔ Drosophila Gr64 (C11 diet, A4); DRD2/DRD4/SLC6A3 ↔ rodent dopamine (C8/C12, A6 Olds–Milner); ADH1B/ALDH2 ↔ Drosophila Adh (D_alcohol); FTO/MC4R ↔ rodent leptin–melanocortin (C11, A4); CADM2 ↔ cross-species impulsivity (A5/A6). The same molecular architecture that produces Sweet Trap signatures in non-human species causally drives human Sweet Trap bitter outcomes. Exposure instruments: n = 258,000–1,331,000 per trait (GCST accessions listed in Methods); outcomes: Finngen R12, n ≈ 413,000, Finnish population.

**图6 | 孟德尔随机化在三个甜蜜陷阱亚类建立遗传因果架构（D层证据）。**

**(a)** 七条因果链的双样本孟德尔随机化森林图，涵盖三个甜蜜陷阱亚类及两个效度验证链。每条链展示三种估计方法：IVW（实心圆）、加权中位数（实心方块）、MR-Egger（三角）。*工程型甜蜜陷阱*（红色）：风险偏好遗传倾向因果提升抑郁风险（IVW OR = 1.38 [1.18, 1.62]，p = 4.9×10⁻⁵，工具变量n=25）和抗抑郁药使用（OR = 1.40 [1.18, 1.65]，p = 9.8×10⁻⁵）；加权中位数一致，MR-Egger截距清洁（p = 0.16, 0.24）。*祖先失配型甜蜜陷阱*（橙色）：饮酒量遗传倾向因果提升酒精性肝病风险5倍（OR = 5.41 [2.76, 10.57]，p = 8.2×10⁻⁷），建立了行为分析无法检测的D_alcohol因果苦涩侧；BMI遗传倾向因果使2型糖尿病风险翻倍（OR = 2.06 [1.61, 2.65]，p = 1.6×10⁻⁸），与果蝇TAS1R/Gr64轴同源（A4，Δ_ST=+0.71）。*判别效度*（**绿色，保护方向**）：受教育年限遗传倾向因果降低抑郁风险（OR = 0.88 [0.81, 0.97]，p = 0.006，n=203），证明教育不是甜蜜陷阱——其F1条件（回报-适应性解耦）未满足。第7链（吸烟→酒精性肝病）MR-Egger截距p=0.009，水平多效性显著，仅在补充材料中报告。**(b)** 敏感性面板：Cochran Q异质性p值与MR-Egger截距p值，7条链中5条Egger截距清洁。**(c)** 跨物种分子桥梁：驱动工具变量的人类GWAS位点与A层动物同源物对应，证明产生非人类物种甜蜜陷阱特征的分子架构同样因果驱动人类苦涩结局，实现跨物种闭环验证。
