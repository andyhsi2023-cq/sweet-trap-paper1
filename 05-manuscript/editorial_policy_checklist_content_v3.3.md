## Section 1 — Statistics and reproducibility
- **Sample size calculation:** Not pre-specified for observational secondary analyses; sample sizes driven by wave availability (Layer B, C) and GWAS public availability (Layer D). Power assessed post hoc: Layer A k = 20 > random-effects floor; Layer D per-chain F-statistic > 30 with ≥ 80% power for OR ≥ 1.1. See Reporting Summary §1.1.
- **Data exclusions:** All pre-registered. See Reporting Summary §1.2.
- **Attempts at replication:** Cross-method robustness (MR 5 methods × 19 chains; spec-curve 3,000 models × 5 focals; discriminant Round 1 + Round 2 blind κ; HRS USA replication of Chinese focal signs).
- **Group allocation (randomization):** Natural randomisation via Mendelian instruments (Layer D).
- **Blinding:** Blinded two-coder rating on discriminant cases; not applicable for other layers (secondary data).
